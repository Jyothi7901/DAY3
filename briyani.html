HYDERABAD BRIYANI

Hyderabadi biryani (also known as Hyderabadi dum biryani) is a style of biryani originating from Hyderabad, India made with basmati rice and meat (mostly mutton). Originating in the kitchens of the Nizam of Hyderabad, it combines elements of Hyderabadi and Mughlai cuisines. Around 1857, the Mughal Empire started declining in Delhi. Around this time, Hyderabad emerged as the center of South Asian culture. This increased several delicious innovations of Hyderabadi Biryani. 

There are many variants of Hyderabadi Biryani, such as:

 1.Hyderabadi Dum Biryani
 2.Hyderabadi Chicken Biryani
 3.Hyderabadi Mutton Biryani
 4.Hyderabadi Veg Dum Biryani
